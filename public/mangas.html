The Best Mangas in 2024

My Top 5 Mangas I Read This Year

Oyasumi Punpun

Very realistic and makes you feel very empty when it ends, but it can also seem somewhat relatable and I loved it so much.

Jagaaaaan

The name is weird but a topnotch action, drama, and romance manga.

The Promised Neverland

The anime doesn't give it credit, the manga is still one of the best I have ever read.

Leviathan

One of the best Action webtoons I have ever read. Better than Solo Leveling and TBATE imo.

She's Hopeless

Not really in to romance Manhwa's, but this made my heart melt.
